How it works

  1. Create your party: Click "Create My Party" and set your party name and the real answer.
  2. Share the link: Send the guest link or QR code to friends and family.
  3. Guests vote: Everyone predicts boy or girl, live from their phones.
  4. Reveal the result: When ready, hit "Reveal Gender" and celebrate together!
